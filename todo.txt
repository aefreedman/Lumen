Guardian
--------
- Blinks
- Press A to Attack
	- AoE
	- Uses energy, radius is determined by some f(radius)
	- Non-linear growth of radius
	- Players can only get hit once per attack
	- Full bar = half the screen, 3 seconds
	- Radius grows as you hold down button
	- Screen shake (grows as you hold down)
	- Guardian can't move while channeling
	- Hits players
	- Max range = mega attack + screen shake + rumble

Crystals
--------
- Light on interact
- Relocate (don't do now)
- Interact 3x to obtain
- Need n number of crystals to win
- Always have n+m crystals on map
- Beat level -> next level n+a crystals (a=1?)

Players
-------
- Blinks
- Has HP
- Takes damage when hit by Guardian's attack
- Needs to collect crystals
	- "Channel" to collect" -> can't move while doing this
	- Release button to cancel
	- Makes sound
	- Makes light (while channeling)
